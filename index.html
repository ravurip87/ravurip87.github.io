Hello, I'm Praseeda Sarvani Ravuri

Hello, my name is Praseeda Sarvani Ravuri, and I’m currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science with a focus on Artificial Intelligence and a minor in Business Administration at Oregon State University.

I have a strong passion for integrating AI with real-world applications, as evidenced by my research experience in cloud computing, NLP, and data science. I’ve collaborated with professors and industry experts on projects that leverage machine learning for intelligent resource management, and I’ve co-authored several research papers in these areas.

In addition to my technical skills, I’m actively involved in leadership roles such as serving as Vice President of Outreach for the Engineering Student Council, and I hold positions in ACM-W and Women in Big Data. These experiences have strengthened my ability to communicate complex ideas, organize large-scale events, and foster a collaborative environment.

Overall, my blend of research, leadership, and technical expertise, combined with my enthusiasm for emerging technologies, has prepared me to contribute meaningfully to innovative projects in AI and software development.
